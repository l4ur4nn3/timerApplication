Self-employed bill calculator

Instructions

  1. Input your hourly rate in the designated field to calculate estimated earnings.
  2. Click the "Start" button to begin the timer.
  3. Use the "Pause" button to take breaks or halt the timer temporarily.
  4. Hit "Reset" to start over and clear all recorded time.
  5. Click on a date on the calendar to add your working time in hh-mm format.
  6. To edit or delete, click on the same date.
  7. Be careful that a deleted entry cannot be recovered.
